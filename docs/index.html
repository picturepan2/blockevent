BlockEvent FAQ

Add Blockchain events list to your calendar

All Blockchain events and industry conferences on BlockEvent.co is available in iCalendar (.ics) format. You can subscribe the calendar in any compatible web calendar or apps and it keeps your calendar in sync.

https://blockevent.co/ical/blockevent.ics

Copy the url above and go to your calendar:

Google Calendar: Find "Add calendar" on the left sidebar and click on the "dots", click "From URL". Then paste the URL into input box and click "Add calendar".

Apple Calendar (macOS App): Click "New Calendar Subscription" under "File" menu. Then paste the URL into input box and click "Subscribe".

Outlook.com Calendar: Click "Discover calendars" on the left sidebar and find "Import calendar", click "From Web". Then paste the URL into input box, type the name "Blockchain Conferences & Events" and click "Import".
